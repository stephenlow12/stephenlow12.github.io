I'm a design graduate from OCAD University in Toronto, Canada. I believe that great design, is just as greatly influenced by the research and empathy towards your users, and the understanding of that human experience.

My profile declares that I'm a product, technology, and basketball enthusiast, but Graphic Design Is My Passion. On my spare time I like to deconstruct iconic logos, or create awesome album covers for emerging musicans.

My first job in design was as an intern at Modulex America, where I produced wayfinding signage for universities, banks, and hospitals. If we go further back, my interest in design probably started around 2005, when I tinkered with Bebo.com (the social network that got away) themes.

I've previously designed experiences at RBC, Ontario Digital Service, SXD Lab, AIR MILES, and Canadian Tire.
