Canadian Tire's Learning Experience Team

UX Research and Design Analyst

I worked two terms on the newly formed Learning Experience team, to help prepare and launch the Triangle Learning Academy, an enterprise-wide internal learning platform.

The Challenge

The Triangle Learning Academy was a project that started early 2017. The idea was to create a hub for corporate learning, where Canadian Tire employees from around the world can experience unique, and industry related, learning oppurtunities.

Along with the TLA, the Canadian Tire onboarding experience was also owned by the Learning Experience team. First impressions are always important, so how do we offer the best welcoming experience for our future employees?

Chat Landing Page

The current Chat Landing page needed an overhaul, as not only was it outdated, but had to redirect users looking for a certain support page to an exteneral support page. The high level goals were to:

  1. • Direct users to the correct vendor support pages.
  2. • Promote use of self-serve options before starting a live chat/call.
  3. • Not hinder or make it difficult for users to start a live chat.
  4. • Follow the current AIR MILES pattern library and branding guidelines.

Ideation

Our client came to us with a rather broad problem. Through research, it was determined that people who travel also want to meet new people, but currently 58% of people reported as 'unhappy' or 'satisfactory' with their ability to 'spontaneously meet new people'.
